- **Important discovery (this run)**: Noticed 3 evaluates `config.if` at *delivery time*, not at *enqueue time*. The `deliver_by.perform_later(notification)` call inside `Noticed::EventJob` always enqueues the delivery job (e.g., `DeliveryMethods::MixinBot.perform_later`); the `config.if` proc is then evaluated inside `Noticed::DeliveryMethod#deliver` when the job runs. Pre-existing `assert_no_enqueued_jobs only: DeliveryMethods::MixinBot` assertions in #1616 (`article_published_notifier_test.rb`) and `comment_deleted_notifier_test.rb` are therefore incorrect — the job IS enqueued even when the guard would skip delivery. Flagged for follow-up; new tests pin the contract at the `recipient_messenger?` level instead.
